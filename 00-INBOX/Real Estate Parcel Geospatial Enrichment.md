# Real Estate Parcel Geospatial Enrichment
## Overview

> [!SOURCE] Sources:
> - *<https://www.perplexity.ai/search/for-the-geospatial-enrichment-JX92wU8rR7Or0SWPoM8.dg>*

This note provides insights into the process and architectural concerns for a system that enriches Real Estate parcel geometry data with necessary geospatial [GIS](Geographic Information System (GIS).md) data, information/insights, and mapping layers for the purpose of assessing, evaluating, performing due diligence, determining the site suitability and related concepts for the parcel being analyzed.
